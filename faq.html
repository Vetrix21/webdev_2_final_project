Frequently Asked Questions

Learn a little more about what we can do to make your experience smoother.

Schedule a service with us now!
  • Where do you operate?
  • Our services expand across all of Illinois. We are hoping soon to branch into neighboring states, such as Wisconsin and Indiana as well.
  • What times are you available to move?
  • How much can I store with your services?
  • Do I need to prepare anything before you show up?
  • That depends on the job, but typically no. A cleared space between the item(s) you want moved and your door would be nice, but we can take care of that as well.
  • How many people can I expect?
  • Again, that depends on the job. Typically, our teams consist of 2-3 pople, but if your job is especially demanding or expedited we can provide more as needed.
  • Do I need to buy my own boxes?
  • We offer boxes as part of our service, for a small charge depending on the type of boxes desired.
  • Can you help me move out of state?
  • So long as the initial job destination is in Illinois, and the drive is no longer than 10 hours to the next location, we can assist you in moving cross-state.
  • How fast are your jobs?
  • Depends on the job, but we aim for speed and efficiency. Expect anywhere from 1-5 hours, or if you have more things to move or are moving houses 5-10 hours.
  • Can I stay home while you work?
  • Of course! We take any measures necesary to ensure we do not intrude on the living spaces of our clients.
  • How much should I expect to pay?
  • We try to be affordable as much as we can. Our hourly rate is $75, though for certain jobs transportation fees may also apply.
  • How can I contact you?
  • You can reach us here, through this website!
