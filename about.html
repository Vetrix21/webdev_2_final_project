A dream that dared to move further.

The twins Jim and Jam Mover had always dreamed of serving others.

From the day their mother had first told them "You're so strong!", the boys knew in their hearts what they should do. Ever since, they have set out to use that strength to help the people around them. After moving houses a couple times, the twins realized their path: movers. Taking not of everything, they came up with their own unique formula and service model to better serve those around them.

Decades of experience

Jim and Jam have been in the business for nearly 30 years now. Starting off with just their dad's pickup truck, they offered to help the locals in their neighborhood, before eventually expanding out into the city, and then all across the state. To say they've made their lives around this would be an understatement. Of course, they can't do it alone. They have hired many capable movers that share their vision, and have trained them to the utmost extent to ensure the satisfaction of the most important person in the business: you, the client.
